      Today we started the morning by meeting in our research lab and discussing what the final week of research would look like. After making progress on that task, our attention shifted to planning our elevator pitch video, which we will present as part of the program. My teammates and I spent a good amount of time brainstorming ideas, discussing different approaches, and creating a script that clearly explains our research in a creative and engaging way. We outlined everyone's roles, organized the flow of the video, and made sure each team member had a clear understanding of what they would be responsible for during filming. By the end of the afternoon, we had developed a solid foundation for the project and felt confident about beginning the recording process tomorrow. It was encouraging to see everyone contributing ideas and working together to create a presentation that represents our research well. Overall, today was a productive planning day that helped prepare our team for a busy and important week ahead.
