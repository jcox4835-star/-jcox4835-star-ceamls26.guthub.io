   Today started much earlier than usual as we woke up around 6:00 a.m. to prepare for our field trip to the Amazon Web Services (AWS) Summit in Washington, D.C. Thanks to Dr. Mack and the Summer Institute staff, our cohort had the opportunity to attend this exciting event and learn more about cloud computing, artificial intelligence, and emerging technologies. We departed campus around 8:15 a.m. and arrived at the convention center around 10:00 a.m., where we checked in, went through security, and received our event badges. Once inside, I explored the exhibition floor with a small group and visited a variety of booths showcasing different AWS technologies and AI-focused companies. It was interesting to learn about the different products, services, and innovations each company was working on, while also seeing how artificial intelligence is being applied across many different industries. Throughout the day, I attended several presentations, fireside chats, and technical sessions that provided a better understanding of Amazon Web Services and the role cloud computing plays in today's technology landscape. Although the event was not focused on recruiting, it was a great opportunity to network, ask questions, and gain insight into different companies and career paths within the technology industry. We also had the chance to collect souvenirs, meet new people, and experience the atmosphere of a large professional technology conference. Overall, the trip was an exciting and educational experience that exposed us to new ideas and demonstrated how the concepts we are learning in the Summer Institute connect to real-world careers and applications.
